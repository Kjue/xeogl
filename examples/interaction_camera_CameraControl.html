CameraControl


controls:
  • mouse click position on entity surface to look at that position
  • mouse shift-click entity to fit it to view
  • mouse click empty space to fit entire scene to view
  • mouse drag or arrow keys to orbit camera
  • keys Z,X - pan forwards and backwards
  • keys A,D - pan left and right
  • keys W,S - pan up and down
  • keys 1,2,3,4,5,6 - select axis-aligned view
  • mouse wheel or '+' and '-' keys to zoom in and out
